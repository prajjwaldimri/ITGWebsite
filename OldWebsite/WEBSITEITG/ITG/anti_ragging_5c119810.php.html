Anti-Ragging Squad:



The Institute also constitutes a smaller body which is known as Anti-ragging Squad to be nominated by the Head of the Institute. The Institute will depute four Anti-ragging Squads which will keep on maintaining vigil, patrolling and shall remain active and alerts at all times. One squad will work for 24 hours.

The Anti-ragging Squad will make surprise raids on Hostels and other places to prevent the potential of ragging. It shall also be the duty of Squad to conduct preliminary enquiry into the incident of ragging referred to it by the Chairman of Anti-ragging Committee.
