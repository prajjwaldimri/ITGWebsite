Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering


The ECE department was started in 2013. The department offers an Under Graduate program in Electronics and Communication engineering. From the very beginning, the ECE department is taken lead in establishing well equipped and modern lab facilities for its students.IT Gopeshwar has become one of the first institutes in Uttarakhand to have National Instruments lab-view laboratory set-up. The LabView laboratory consist of National Instruments hardware like data acquisition cards,signal conditioning units along with the latest version of National Instruments LabView software,vision Development Module, Signal Express, NI Matrix , Lab Windows, Measurement Studio and toolkits etc.
