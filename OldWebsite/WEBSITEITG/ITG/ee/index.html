Department of Electrical Engineering


Welcome to the Department of Electrical Engineering at Institute of Technology, Gopeshwar. The Electrical Engineering Department was established during the inception of the institute in 2013. The department is committed to the advancement of knowledge in core electrical engineering. It has a vibrant academic environment and has a broad based curriculum offering modern courses. The Department has well equipped laboratories such as Electrical Machines laboratory, Control Systems laboratory, Electrical Measurements laboratory, Power Electronics & Drives Laboratory (well equipped with NI instruments), Electrical Workshop, Computer laboratory and Switchgear &Protection laboratory. Department has a fine blend of young and dynamic faculty involved in providing quality education at Undergraduate level. The major areas of faculty expertise include Power Systems, Electric Drives, and Control& Instrumentation. The lab facilities and the infrastructure are regularly upgraded and are well supported by the institute and the industry. The Department emphasizes on strong Industry
