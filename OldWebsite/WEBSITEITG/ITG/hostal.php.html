HOSTEL

Institute of Technology, Gopeshwar established in 2013 has currently four hostels for boys and girls in PPP mode at chamoli. Based on the principle of "Homes away from home", hostels available in the campus provide all the basic facilities to the students with proper care and tender of the administrative and supporting staff. Institute has residential facilities for more than 200 hundred students in the hostel. Institute has three boys and one girls hostels, having rooms available on sharing basis (two/three seater) accommodating maximum institute students in comfortable and conducive manner. The detail of the hostels along with their capacity is as follows:

S.No. Name of Hostel Type No. of Rooms No. of Seats Strength of the Hostel
1. Nirmal Palace Girls 22 2/3 seater 60
2. Basant Lodge Boys 10 2/3 seater 40
3. Basundhara Lodge Boys 10 2/3seater 40
4. Himani Lodge Boys 11 2/3seater 55

A year-wise hostel system is followed for allocating the hostel rooms to the students. Each hostel has been equipped with water purifiers, water coolers, Geyser for hot water and telephone etc. All hostels are fully connected with emergency/continuous power supply through DG set. Each hostel has installed sufficient security cameras for necessary security check and surveillance. A centralised mess facility is provided to the students residing in the campus. Every boarder is provided with a chair, a table, a cot and a locker. The hostels are close to the academic area and playing grounds. Thus, the students get ample opportunities to avail all the indoor and outdoor games facilities such as Carom Board, Chess, Table Tennis, Volleyball and Badminton. A gymnasium is also available for the students to maintain their fitness.

For the smooth-functioning of daily routines and proper care of girls in Girls' Hostel, a full time matron lives in the Girls' Hostel. Apart from matron, two other female faculty members also live there. Matron takes good care of health issues of girls residing in the hostel along with the nurses available in the Institute Dispensary for 24 hours.

Due care is being taken regarding the health of the students residing in the hostel. Institute vehicle is available for 24hrs to them without any charges for medical check-up/treatment. In case of emergency (during late night hours), the boarders can use this facility to visit district hospital.

All the hostels are administered by a team of a Chief Warden with Wardens, Associate Wardens, AR (Hostel) and senior students as Prefect. The Chief Warden and a team of Wardens are always sincere in improving the facilities and environment of the hostels. Their main role is to coordinate the working of all the hostels, maintain the discipline in the hostels and take decisions on all the policy matters related to the hostels.

Being a institute of National level, students come from all the parts of India and so in hostels they rejoice almost all the festivals celebrated across the country such as Ganesh Utsav, Diwali, Lohri, Holi, Gudi padwa, Independence Day, and so on. These festivals bring tremendous enthusiasm and energy among the hostel inmates which creates a friendly bonding, a unity in team spirit, great opportunity in network building as well as socio cultural interaction and harmony among them.
