Institute of Technology, Gopeshwar

Institute of Technology, Gopeshwar is one of the Four constituent colleges of Uttarakhand Technical University that was established in October 2013 with the approval of Uttarakhand Government. IT Gopeshwar,is an Institute which aims to promote technological advancement in the state of uttarakhand. It provides such a conductive environment to his students in which they become intellectually capable, innovative and entrepreneurial professionals. IT Gopeshwar also provides an educational training that promotes and emphasizes social awareness as well as development of professional skills among the students. Here the knowledge of the students is broadened, deepened and enriched as the institute provide such solutions that benefit humanity and the natural environment. IT Gopeshwar offers B.Tech. Degree programs in the in the field of following streams: 1. Civil Engineering 2. Computer Science and Engineering 3. Electrical Engineering 4. Electronics and Communication Engineering 5. Mechanical Engineering
