Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand

IT Gopeshwar, an Institute which aims to promote technological advancement in Chamoli district of Garhwal region endows with his students an environment in which they become intellectually capable, innovative and entrepreneurial professionals. IT Gopeshwar also provides an educational training that promotes and emphasizes social awareness as well as development of professional skills among the students. Here the knowledge of the students is broadened, deepened and enriched as the institute provide such solutions that benefit humanity and the natural environment. IT Gopeshwar offers B.Tech. program in the following branches 1. Civil Engineering 2. Computer Science and Engineering 3. Electrical Engineering 4. Electronics and Communication Engineering 5. Mechanical Engineering
