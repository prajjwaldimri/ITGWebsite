NIT Uttarakhand and IIT Bombay Joint Collaboration for Open Source Software


An educational workshop on "Open Source Software - IT for you" was organised on 2nd August, 2014 in NIT Uttarakhand at Srinagar, Garhwal. This workshop was arranged under the Spoken Tutorial Project initiated by Ministry of Human Resource Development, Govt. of India and executed by IIT Bombay. In this workshop, Ms. Shyama Iyer and Ms. Akanksha Saini, experts from IIT Bombay, delivered lectures on the use of non-commercial softwares. These softwares mainly contribute towards value addition to class room teaching and training. In coming days, the students having the knowledge of and expertise in these softwares will have very bright job prospects. About 400 students and 50 faculty members of NIT Uttarakhnad participated in this workshop. In next 15 days, NIT Uttarakhand will have liasoning with IIT Bombay for Spoken Tutorials, under this project. Mr. Krishan Kumar Assistance Professor in CSE and Mr. Nitin Kumar HoD CSE and other faculty member of CSE department of NIT Uttarakhnad took special efforts to make the workshop a success.
