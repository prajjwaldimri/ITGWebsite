Procedure to Pay Fee (Second Semester and beyond)


First, click on the URL given on the Institute website. That will directly link you to the SBI website. Select your category of payment: 'Students fees with hostel' or 'Students fees without hostel'. Then enter your roll no and click on submit. That will direct you to the next page and you have to enter your name, DoB, mobile no. and the text image to proceed to the next step. Verify your details as displayed and click on confirm for payment. You can pay fees through SBI net banking which will charge Rs.10/- only and other bank's net banking will charge Rs. 15/-. You can also pay through SBI ATM-cum-Debit Card which will charge Rs. 10/-. Other bank's Credit and Debit cards will charge Rs. 818/-. Take the printout of payment receipt after payment of fees. You can also pay the fees at any SBI branch by taking the printout of the challan. For that purpose you have to click on SBI Branch under 'Other Payment Modes' on the same page and save the pre-acknowledge form and print it before going to branch. The depositor copy will be given to you after payment of fees at SBI branch. Then, you have to update the payment status by clicking the same link again displayed on the website. At the left side, you can see 'Payment History' and click on that. That will direct you to the next page where you have to select the right side and are required to fill up challan reference no. & DoB or mobile no. followed by click on 'Go'. That will complete your payment of fees. Fees cannot be paid via ATM.
