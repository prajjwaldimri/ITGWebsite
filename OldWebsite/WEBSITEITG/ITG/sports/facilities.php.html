Sports Facilities

Institution provides the following indoor and outdoor sports facilities of various games and sports.

The sports arena is near the boy's hostel and is also with well-equipped facilities. So the players can practice at night time also. Students have opportunity to play at any time to have fun. Institute hires the indoor stadium & ground for practice and organizing the tournament from the other Institute. Overall, the institute has sufficient facilities for games and sports.IT Gopeshwar has a sports council to coordinate all the sports activities. In spite of being a new college, the Institute has been successful in developing a firm foundation in the field of sports.
