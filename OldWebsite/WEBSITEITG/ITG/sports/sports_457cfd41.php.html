Facilities / Volleyball

Volleyball is one of the most played games at NIT Uttarakhand. The ground is close to the first year boys' hostel and is also equipped with flood lights so that players can practice at night time also. Besides participating in the Inter-IIT Sports meets, the team also plays practice matches regularly against the local teams such as Sashastra Seema Bal, HNB Garhwal University, Polytechnic etc. This helps in increasing the skills of players and at the same time provides them with an opportunity to interact with the local people.
